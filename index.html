Favourite Discovery 🤍

While making this website, I realised something strange.

I don't actually know you that well.

I don't know your comfort food. I don't know which ride you'd run toward first at a fair. I don't know which bench you'd choose in a park. I don't know which store you'd walk into before all the others.

I don't know whether you text people immediately after waking up or only after you've fully started your day. I don't even know when you fall asleep half the time, even though I've definitely waited around trying to figure it out.

And somehow, that became the hardest part of making this.

Not because I'm upset about any of it. Not because I think I should already know.

But because I wanted to make something special for you, and I kept realising how many blank spaces still exist.

There are no years of memories here. No old photographs. No ridiculous stories we've been telling for ages. No shared moment that instantly makes us laugh the second it's mentioned.

Just a collection of small things I've learned about you so far.

And maybe that's what this really is. Not a record of everything I know about you. Just proof that I'm still curious to learn the rest.

I know people aren't meant to be understood all at once. The best things usually take time.

Still, somewhere between building this page and rewriting it for the hundredth time, I realised that this is only the beginning of the story.

And strangely enough, that thought scares me a little. But it also makes me smile.
